Who We Are

About Smart EA

Smart EA was built by traders, for traders. We wanted a way to trade gold algorithmically without needing to be glued to a screen — so we built it ourselves and made it available to everyone.

Our Mission

We believe algorithmic trading shouldn't be reserved for hedge funds and institutions. Smart EA gives retail traders access to a professional-grade automated system that trades XAU/USD on MetaTrader 5 — running 24/5 in the cloud, with no technical knowledge required.

Built on Real Data

Every strategy is backtested on real tick data with 100% history quality before it ever touches a live account.

Always On

Smart EA runs entirely in the cloud. No VPS, no leaving your device on — just connect and let it trade.

Your Capital, Your Control

We never touch your funds. Smart EA connects to your own broker account — you stay in full control at all times.

Simple Setup

Get up and running in minutes with no technical knowledge needed. Just connect your MT5 account and activate your licence.
