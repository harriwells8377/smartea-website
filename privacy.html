Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address — collected at purchase for licence delivery and support.
  • MT5 account credentials — used solely to connect your account for automated trading. We do not store your password.
  • Usage data — basic analytics to improve the platform (e.g. page visits, feature usage).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver and activate your licence.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To send important service updates and renewal reminders.
  • To improve Smart EA's features and performance.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We use trusted third-party services solely to operate our platform (e.g. payment processing via Stripe). These providers are bound by their own privacy policies.

4. Your MT5 Credentials

Your broker login credentials are transmitted securely and are never stored on our servers. They are used only to establish the trading connection and are encrypted in transit.

5. Data Retention

We retain your email and order information for as long as your account is active or as required for legal and billing purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies for basic analytics and to improve your browsing experience. No tracking cookies are used for advertising purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@smarteatrading.com

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify active users of any significant changes via email.
